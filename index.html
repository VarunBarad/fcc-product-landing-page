Why I made this?

I was frazzled with having to keep track of my attendance in different subjects every semester to avoid getting detention. I started with a record of my attended classes in a notebook. Eventually I made an app for it.

Features

  • Easily mark off whether you attended any class on a particular day
  • Completely editable history to enter any attendance later
  • Widgets to simplify recording attendance
  • Custom threshold of required attendance per class
